What are the system requirements?

The system requirements for Call of Duty are...


OS: Windows 10 64 Bit (latest update)


CPU: Intel Core i3-6100 / Core i5-2500K or AMD Ryzen 3 1200.


RAM: 8 GB. Hi-Rez Assets Cache: Up to 32 GB.


Video Card: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 or AMD Radeon RX 470. Video Memory: 2 GB.
